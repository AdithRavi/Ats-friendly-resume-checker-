# 🔍 ATS Scan Pro | Resume Intelligence
**Beating the bot to get the interview.**

As a student at GEC Barton Hill, I noticed that many of my talented peers were getting rejected by MNCs before a human even saw their resume. I built **ATS Scan Pro** to demystify the Applicant Tracking System (ATS) process and help engineers optimize their applications for the digital age.

## 💡 Why I built this
Most big companies (Accenture, Google, TATA) use automated filters to rank candidates based on keyword frequency. This tool allows you to:
* **Benchmark:** Compare your resume text against a specific Job Description.
* **Identify Gaps:** Instantly see which technical keywords (like 'SolidWorks', 'Python', or 'UX Research') are missing from your profile.
* **Iterate:** Fine-tune your wording until you hit a high match score.

## 🛠️ How it works
* **Keyword Mapping:** Uses a custom JavaScript engine to scan and cross-reference text strings.
* **UX-First Design:** A clean, split-screen interface inspired by modern enterprise dashboards.
* **Instant Feedback:** No waiting—the score and missing tags update immediately upon scanning.

## 🚀 How to use
1. Paste your Resume content on the left.
2. Paste the Job Description on the right.
3. Hit **Analyze** and see where you can improve.
